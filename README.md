# Heart Disease Analysis – SmartBridge Project

## Overview
This project analyzes heart disease risk factors using Tableau. The goal is to identify key risk factors such as smoking, BMI, age, and gender.

## Tools Used
- Tableau Public
- SQLite
- Python (for data cleaning)

## Dataset
- Source: Heart_new2.csv
- Records: 4500
- Fields: 18

## Visualizations
- 10 charts including:
  - Gender vs Heart Disease
  - Age vs Heart Disease
  - Smoking vs Heart Disease
  - BMI vs Heart Disease
  - Diabetes vs Heart Disease
  - Physical Activity vs Heart Disease
  - Alcohol vs Heart Disease
  - Stroke vs Heart Disease
  - Age vs Mental Health
  - General Health vs Heart Disease

## Dashboard
Interactive dashboard with filters for Age, Gender, and Smoking.

## Story
4 scenes: Introduction, Key Risk Factors, Demographic Insights, Conclusion.

## Key Insights
- Smoking and high BMI are major risk factors.
- Older age groups show higher heart disease rates.
- Men show higher rates than women.
- Poor general health is strongly associated with heart disease.

## Recommendations
- Stop smoking
- Maintain healthy weight
- Exercise regularly
- Get regular health checkups
