Project Documentation – Heart Disease Analysis

1. Problem Statement: Heart disease is a leading cause of death worldwide. This project uses Tableau to identify key risk factors such as smoking, BMI, age, and gender.

2. Data Source: Heart_new2.csv (4500 records, 18 fields)

3. Tools Used: Tableau Public, SQLite

4. Visualizations Created: 10 charts (Gender, Age, Smoking, BMI, Diabetes, Physical Activity, Alcohol, Stroke, Mental Health, General Health)

5. Dashboard: Interactive dashboard with filters for Age, Gender, and Smoking.

6. Story: 4 scenes – Introduction, Key Risk Factors, Demographic Insights, Conclusion.

7. Key Insights:
   - Smoking and high BMI are major risk factors.
   - Older age groups (55-69) show higher heart disease rates.
   - Men show higher rates than women.
   - Poor general health is strongly associated with heart disease.

8. Recommendations: Stop smoking, maintain healthy weight, exercise regularly, and get regular health checkups.
